13 Scholarship for International Students in Canada 2023

Author By admin Categories Student Visa

Do you wish to begin your educational journey in Canada? Happy news There are several scholarships available in Canada for foreign students. In this article, we’ll go into great detail about the scholarships available at the best institutions in Canada, their advantages, and how to apply.

For the academic year 2023–2024, major colleges in Canada are offering more than 800 scholarships to foreign students. These scholarships cover tuition, housing costs, health insurance, and travel expenses in addition to an average monthly stipend of $2500.

If you are interested in earning Canada PR for the future, this will go well for you. Fill out an application for completely financed scholarships and realize your dream of attending one of the best colleges in the world, such as the University of Waterloo, the University of Montréal, Douglas College, the University of Saskatchewan, the University of Toronto, and many others.

13 Top Scholarships for International Students in Canada

  • University of Waterloo Scholarships

Institution: University of Waterloo

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Undergraduate/Masters / PhD

International students may apply for the University of Waterloo Scholarship, which is completely financed. This fellowship is available for studies at the undergrad, master’s, and doctoral levels. A $10,000 grant is available from Waterloo University.

  • Université de Montréal Scholarships

Institution: Université de Montréal

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Undergraduate/Masters / PhD

The Université de Montréal Scholarships exempt recipients from paying tuition. All international students are welcome to submit an application. These scholarships are offered for studies at the undergraduate, master’s, and PhD levels. Scholarships from the University of Montréal will cover up to $27,300 annually.

  • Douglas College International Student Scholarships

Campus: Douglas College

Study in: Canada

Study Level: Undergraduate

International undergraduate students may apply for the Douglas College International Student Scholarship. This scholarship will pay for up to CAD 7000 in tuition. Each academic year, a maximum of 5 qualified grantees will be chosen.

  • University of Saskatchewan Graduate Scholarship

Campus:University of Saskatchewan

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Masters / PhD

A fully financed master’s and PhD scholarship is available to international students through the University of Saskatchewan. This scholarship offers up to $16,000 for master’s degrees and $20,000 for PhDs.

  • Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship Program

Institution:University of Toronto

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Undergraduate

A fully funded scholarship is available to international students through the Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship Program 2022. For undergraduate studies, there are a total of 37 scholarships available. The four years of full housing support, tuition, books, and incidentals are all covered by this scholarship.

  • UBC Undergraduate Scholarships

Institution:University of British Columbia (UBC)

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Undergraduate

UBC offers two different kinds of undergraduate scholarships: the Outstanding International Student (OIS) Award and the International Major Entrance Scholarships (IMES). Depending on the amount of funding available, these overseas scholarships fluctuate in quantity and level each year.

  • Canadian Francophonie Scholarship Program

Institution: CFSP

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Masters / PhD

Scholarships from the University of Montréal exempt recipients from paying tuition. The application process is open to all international students. These scholarships are offered for undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral study. Up to $27,300 will be given annually through Université de Montréal Scholarships.

  • Dalhousie University Scholarships

Institution: Dalhousie University

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Masters / PhD

A fully financed scholarship for international students is the Dalhousie University Scholarship. This award is available for graduate and postdoctoral work. The $30,000 scholarship provides a monthly income, housing expenses, fee waivers, and health insurance.

  • University Canada West Scholarships

Institution: University Canada West

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Undergraduate/Masters

International students may apply for the University Canada West Scholarship, which is supported. This grant is exclusively available for graduate and undergraduate study. For undergraduate degrees, this grant offers up to $20,000, and for master’s degrees, up to $10,000.

  • McGill University Scholarships

Institution: McGill University

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Undergraduate/Masters / PhD

International students might receive fully financed scholarships from McGill University. This fellowship is available for studies at undergrad, master’s, and doctoral levels. For undergraduate students, this scholarship offers up to $8,500, and for master’s and doctoral students, it offers modest bursaries.

  • Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship 

Institution: Canadian institution with a quota to host Vanier scholars

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: PhD

International students may apply for the fully financed Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship Program. For PhD studies, this scholarship is available. This scholarship has a three-year value of $50,000 per year.

  • SheBegan International Scholarship for Women

Institution: She Began for Women

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Undergraduate/Masters / PhD

A fully financed scholarship for international students is available through the She Began for Women Scholarship 2021. This award is available for studies in undergrad, master’s, and doctoral programs. Scholarship perks include health insurance, a monthly stipend, and degree sponsorship ranging from $18,000 to $32,000 USD.

  • University of Calgary Scholarships

Institution: University of Calgary

Study in: Canada

Level of Study: Undergraduate

A completely financed undergraduate award is the University of Calgary International Entrance Scholarship. The following financial aid packages are available to international students beginning their first year of study in the Fall Term: $60,000, $15,000 (renewable annually), and $10,000 (non-renewable).
